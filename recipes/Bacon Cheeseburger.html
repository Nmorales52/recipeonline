Bacon Cheeseburger

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons fine chopped onions
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon steak sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon cider vinegar
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 4 slices sharp cheddar cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns, split, buttered, and toasted
  • 8 cooked beef bacon strips
  • Additional Seasonings – These extra seasonings (lettuce leaves, shredded lettuce, tomato slices, red onion slices)

Description

You see these everywhere in America, especially on Independence Day! Where can you get a juicy burger that is so superior to drive-thru fare? Right in your backyard with this delicious bacon cheeseburger recipe.

Recipe credits:

  • Taste of Home - Blog

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the first seven ingredients. Crumble beef over mixture and mix well. Shape into four patties.
  2. Grill burgers, covered, over medium heat or broil 3 in. from the heat for 4-7 minutes on each side or until a thermometer reads 160° and juices run clear.
  3. Top with cheese. Grill 1 minute longer or until cheese is melted. Serve on buns with bacon and toppings of your choice.
  4. Serve and Enjoy!
